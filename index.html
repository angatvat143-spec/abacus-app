The Cart Story

It started with one cart,
one corner, and a flask of cold brew.

No lounge. No menu cards. Just a guy who knew coffee, parked at a street corner in Vesu, serving people who wanted something better than instant and faster than a café queue.

Fifteen years of pulling shots taught us what actually matters: the coffee has to be honest, the wait has to be short, and the price has to make sense. To Go Coffee grew up — but we never moved indoors in spirit. We're still the cart. Just a faster, sharper one.
